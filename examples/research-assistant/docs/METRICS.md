# Metrics

Quantitative tracking for research effectiveness.

## Quality Scale

| Rating | Meaning | Criteria |
|--------|---------|----------|
| 5/5 | Exceptional | Comprehensive synthesis, novel connections, highly cited sources |
| 4/5 | Strong | Good coverage, clear synthesis, reliable sources |
| 3/5 | Adequate | Answers question, some gaps, decent sources |
| 2/5 | Weak | Incomplete, unclear, questionable sources |
| 1/5 | Failed | Wrong answer, major gaps, or unreliable |
