# Iteration Loop

## Overview

Each interaction or planned outreach is one iteration. The goal is to maintain and deepen relationships thoughtfully.

---

## Phase 1: REVIEW (10 min)

### Actions
- [ ] Check contacts due for outreach
- [ ] Note upcoming dates (birthdays, anniversaries)
- [ ] Review recent interactions and outcomes
- [ ] Identify any context triggers (news, milestones)

### Contact Status Check
| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Overdue | Past ideal contact date | Prioritize outreach |
| Due soon | Within ideal window | Plan outreach |
| Recent | Recently connected | No action needed |
| New context | Life event or trigger | Consider reaching out |

---

## Phase 2: SELECT (5 min)

### Actions
- [ ] Choose who to contact based on priority
- [ ] Review their profile and history
- [ ] Note last interaction outcome
- [ ] Identify appropriate approach

### Selection Criteria
1. **Urgency**: Overdue or time-sensitive
2. **Opportunity**: Natural reason to reach out
3. **Relationship tier**: Higher tiers get priority
4. **Balance**: Don't neglect any tier too long

---

## Phase 3: PREPARE (10-15 min)

### Actions
- [ ] Review person's profile
- [ ] Check previous conversation notes
- [ ] Identify topic or reason for outreach
- [ ] Choose communication channel

### Profile Review
- Name, nickname, pronouns
- Relationship context (how you know them)
- Recent life events
- Communication preferences
- Topics they care about
- Previous conversation threads

---

## Phase 4: INTERACT (varies)

### Actions
- [ ] Execute outreach
- [ ] Be present in the interaction
- [ ] Note new information shared
- [ ] End appropriately

### Interaction Types
| Type | Typical Duration | Examples |
|------|-----------------|----------|
| Quick check-in | 5-10 min | Text, quick call |
| Catch-up | 30-60 min | Video call, coffee |
| Deep connection | 2+ hours | Dinner, activity |
| Passive | N/A | React to their post, share article |

---

## Phase 5: RECORD (10 min)

### Actions
- [ ] Update contact record
- [ ] Log interaction details
- [ ] Note any new information
- [ ] Record follow-up commitments

### Interaction Log Entry
```markdown
## Interaction: [Date]

**Contact**: [Name]
**Type**: [Quick/Catch-up/Deep/Passive]
**Channel**: [Text/Call/In-person/etc.]
**Duration**: [Time]

### Summary
[What was discussed]

### New Information Learned
- [Personal detail]
- [Life update]
- [Preference]

### Follow-ups Committed
- [ ] [What you said you'd do]

### How It Went
[Quality: 1-5, notes on reception]
```

---

## Phase 6: REFLECT (5 min)

### Actions
- [ ] Assess interaction quality
- [ ] Note what worked/didn't work
- [ ] Update approach preferences
- [ ] Set next contact target

### Reflection Questions
- Did they seem happy to hear from me?
- Was my reason for reaching out appropriate?
- What topics resonated?
- What should I remember for next time?

---

## Phase 7: CAPTURE (5 min)

### Actions
- [ ] Log metrics in METRICS.md
- [ ] Update LEARNING.md if insight gained
- [ ] Update contact record with preferences
- [ ] Schedule next outreach window

---

## Cycle Frequencies

### Every Interaction (Micro)
- Complete full loop
- Log metrics

### Weekly (Meso)
- Review all overdue contacts
- Check upcoming dates
- Assess tier coverage

### Monthly (Macro)
- Review relationship health by tier
- Identify neglected connections
- Update contact priorities
- Clean up stale contacts
