# Metrics

Quantitative tracking for relationship management effectiveness.

---

## Quality Scale

| Rating | Meaning | Criteria |
|--------|---------|----------|
| 5/5 | Exceptional | Deep connection, mutual value, growth |
| 4/5 | Strong | Good interaction, positive reception |
| 3/5 | Adequate | Completed outreach, neutral response |
| 2/5 | Weak | Forced, awkward, or poor timing |
| 1/5 | Failed | Ignored, negative reaction, or boundary crossed |
