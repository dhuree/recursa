## Outreach Timing

### When to Reach Out
- `[P]` Birthdays always warrant a message
- `[*]` Monday mornings get poor response rates
- `[*]` After they post about achievements is good timing
- `[-]` Friday afternoons work for casual check-ins
- `[?]` Seasonal messages (holidays) may feel impersonal

### Cadence Patterns
- `[*]` Close friends: every 1-2 weeks feels right
- `[*]` Professional contacts: quarterly minimum
- `[-]` Acquaintances: twice a year may be enough
- `[?]` Weekly is too much for most relationships

## Communication Approaches

### What Works
- `[P]` Personalization dramatically improves reception
- `[*]` Asking about something specific they mentioned works
- `[*]` Sharing relevant articles appreciated by professionals
- `[-]` Voice memos feel more personal than text

### What Doesn't Work
- `[*]` Generic "how are you?" gets poor response
- `[*]` Too many back-to-back messages feels needy
- `[-]` Calling without warning annoys some people

## Relationship Maintenance

### What Strengthens Connections
- `[P]` Remembering and following up on their concerns
- `[*]` Celebrating their wins publicly (when appropriate)
- `[-]` Offering help before being asked
- `[?]` Small thoughtful gifts may be meaningful

### What Weakens Connections
- `[*]` Only reaching out when you need something
- `[*]` Forgetting important dates repeatedly
- `[-]` Being slow to respond to their outreach

---

## Recovery Patterns

### Reviving Cold Relationships
- `[*]` Acknowledge the gap honestly
- `[-]` Reference shared positive memory
- `[?]` News about mutual connection as opener

### After a Miss
- `[-]` Apologize briefly, don't over-explain
- `[?]` Make next interaction extra thoughtful

## Group Dynamics

### Managing Overlapping Circles
- `[-]` Be careful sharing info between mutual connections
- `[?]` Group outings may strengthen multiple relationships
